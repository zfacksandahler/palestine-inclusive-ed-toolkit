# Palestine Inclusive Education Toolkit

## Project Goal
This repository hosts a comprehensive resource kit designed to support educators, policymakers, and stakeholders in implementing inclusive education practices for students with disabilities in Palestine. The toolkit provides practical templates, glossaries, and checklists that can be adapted to local contexts.

## Target Audience
- Teachers and school administrators in the West Bank and Gaza
- Education policymakers and ministry officials
- Non-governmental organizations (NGOs) working in special education
- Teacher trainers and university faculty
- Parents and community advocates

## Intended Contents
1. **Policy Brief Template** – A structured template for advocating inclusive education policies
2. **Glossary of Key Terms** – Essential terminology in English and Arabic with definitions
3. **Best Practices Checklist** – Actionable steps for classroom implementation
4. **Case Studies** – Real-world examples from Palestinian schools (future)
5. **Resource Directory** – Local and international support organizations (future)

## How to Contribute
